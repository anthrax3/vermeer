Mauritshuis

Three Vermeers are in the Mauritshuis in The Hague, Netherlands. I got there by train from Amsterdam.

Train

I took the train from the Central station in Amsterdam. There are ticket machines for the national rail service and it’s fairly easy to select a ticket from there to the central station in the Hague with a return at anytime later the same day.

The train ride is about 50 minutes and you pass through a few towns and on my trip I happened to see some tulip fields.

Mauritshuis

Once you’re in The Hague it’s about a 10m minute walk from the central station to the Mauritshuis
