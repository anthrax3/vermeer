About Why

Vermeer is an artist and I like his paintings. There are only 34 or so known to exist and so I decided to see them everytime they were in a city I was in. There is a photo per visit here but many places have more than one vermeer. At this point I've seen sixteen.

About Site

I created a spreadsheet with info about my visits to Vermeers as well as a URL for the image for each vsit. This site uses sheetsee.js and tabletop.js to pull the data from the spreadsheet and display it on the site.
